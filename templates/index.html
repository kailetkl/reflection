About

We are the first business school in Singapore, and the third in Asia, to be accredited by both the EFMD Quality Improvement System (EQUIS) and Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB). These are the most widely recognised quality assurance standards for accounting and business education, and they assure that degrees we confer are recognised around the world.â€‹


Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB)

We have been accredited by the AACSB for both business and accounting since 2005. AACSB accreditation is a hallmark of excellence in management education. It assures quality and promotes excellence and continuous improvement in undergraduate and graduate education for business education and accounting.

EFMD Quality Improvement System (EQUIS)

We have been conferred full EQUIS accreditation since 2004. The EQUIS quality assurance scheme is a leading international system of quality assessment, improvement, and accreditation of higher education institutions in management and business administration.
